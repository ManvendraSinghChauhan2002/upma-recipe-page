Today, we will be learning how to make Upma!

Ingredients needed (Below ingredients is for 1-2 katori Upma)

  • Suji/Rawa:1/2 bowl
  • Tomatoes: 2 (finely chopped)
  • Onions: 2 (finely chopped)
  • Green chillies: 3
  • Turmeric powder:1/2 tbsp
  • Coriander powder: 1 tbsp
  • Red chilli powder: 1/4 tbsp
  • Chana daal: 1 tbsp
  • Urad daal: 1 tbsp
  • Mustard oil: 1 serving spoon
  • mustard seeds: 1/2 tbsp
  • Water: Take 1.5 bowls of water if you want less thick upma and take 1 bowl if you want thick upma (In upma the ratio of Suji to water is 1:2 to 1:3 means for every 1 part of suji we need 2-3 parts of water.
  • Curry leaves: a handful
  • Salt:1/2 tbsp or as per taste
  • Chopped vegetables like cauliflower, etc (optional but benificial for health)
  • My blessings that it will taste great!

Cooking Time!

  1. Dry roast suji on medium flame until it becomes golden-brown also you have to use a spatula to move the suji else some part of it will get burned and some will not even cook then place it aside.
  2. Turn on gas on lowest flame. Put cumin, let it roast, once it's done, put chana and urad daal and move using spatula. Now put onions and let them turn brown then put green chillies and then tomatoes and stir and mix everything well.
  3. Now its time for spices!. So, first add salt then turmeric then coriander then red chilli mix well. Now you can add vegetables like cauliflower, carrots, etc. if you want.
  4. Now put water in the wok in which you have prepared the vegetables and let the water boil by turning the gas on high flame. When big bubbles start appearing the water is boiled.
  5. So, now put suji in it but no need to stir this time also when the water was boiling (the previous step). Now, you will see your upma is cooked turn off the flame based on how thick upma you want.
  6. Your upma is ready to eat!
